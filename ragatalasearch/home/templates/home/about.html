About Raga Tala Search

Raga and Tala Search made easy!

Raga

There are about 950 ragas that you can search here in the following ways:

Raga Search by Name: Search ragas by entering the raga name

Raga Search by Arohanam: Click on the swaras of the arohanam that you want to search and find the raga from the list of ragas

Raga Search by Avarohanam Click on the swaras forming a avarohanam

Also,

Melakarta table: Click on the ragas in the melakarta table to get the details of the mela raga and its janya ragas

Tala

Search from the list of talas including the 35 talas, Ashtotara shata(108) talas and Mela talas

Tala search by Name: Enter the tala name

Tala search by aksharas: Get the tala name and angams by entering aksharas

Also,

Tala tables consisting of 35 Talas table , Ashtotara shata(108) talas table and Mela talas table.
